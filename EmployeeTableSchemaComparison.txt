## ============================ Key Differences ============================

### 1. Multi-Tenancy
OLD: No Tenant Support. All Models Use Direct FK To auth.User.
NEW: Every Model Extends TenantScopedModel (tenant_id FK + workspace_id FK).

### 2. Employee Identification
OLD: Profile.atg_id (CharField, unique=True)
NEW: EmployeeProfile.employee_code (CharField, unique per tenant)

### 3. Pay Amounts
OLD: IntegerField (whole INR, no decimals)
NEW: DecimalField(max_digits=14, decimal_places=2)

### 4. Leave Management
OLD: Single FloatField on Profile (leaves_wallet) + Leave model in mainapp
NEW: LeavePolicy + LeaveBalance + LeaveTransaction + LeaveRequest (MainApp)

### 5. Status Tracking
OLD: UserStatus (OneToOne, mutable - overwritten)
NEW: UserStatusSnapshot (FK, immutable history with effective_from/to)

### 6. Bank Details
OLD: BankDetails (plain account numbers)
NEW: EmployeeBankAccount (masked numbers + verification_status + fund_account_ref)

### 7. Payment Tracking
OLD: PayData + PaymentData (IntegerFields)
NEW: PayProfile + EmployeePaymentSnapshot (DecimalFields + performance_pay)

### 8. Department-Employee Link
OLD: Works_in (separate join table to User)
NEW: DepartmentMembership (to EmployeeProfile, with status/start/end dates)

### 9. Position-Employee Link
OLD: Emp_position (separate join table via username)
NEW: EmployeeProfile.position (direct FK)

### 10. Skill Proficiency
OLD: UserSkill.rating (0-10)
NEW: UserSkill.proficiency (1=Basic, 2=Intermediate, 3=Advanced) + rating (0-10)

### 11. Goals
OLD: No title field, description + assigned_on only
NEW: Goal has title, description, due_on, status, metadata

### 12. Certificates
OLD: Certificates (position as string)
NEW: EmployeeCertificate (position_title + storage_reference + issued_on)

### 13. Interview Progress
OLD: InternInterviewProgress (User FK, no job_id)
NEW: InterviewProgress (EmployeeProfile FK + job_id + metadata)

### 14. Effort Reports
OLD: UserEffortReport (FK to Project model)
NEW: UserEffortReport (FK to EmployeeProfile, project_reference string)

### 15. Onboarding Offers
OLD: NewProvisionalEmployeeMail (email-based, many bool fields)
NEW: OnboardingOffer (full lifecycle: Draft->Issued->Accepted, offer_payload JSON)

### 16. Leave Requests
OLD: Leave (department FK, no policy, no approval stages)
NEW: LeaveRequest (approval_stage, approved_by, approval_payload JSON)
