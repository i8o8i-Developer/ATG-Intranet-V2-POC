TERMS AND CONDITIONS

Work Structure & Internship Tenure

Your engagement with Banao Technologies will be structured as a performance-driven internship for a duration of 6 months based on performance, consistency, and business requirements. This internship does not guarantee full-time employment; however, high-performing candidates may be considered for a full-time role upon completion.

The role follows a hybrid, execution-focused work model and involves active participation in client-facing activities such as meetings, events, and on-ground engagements. While flexibility is provided, you may be required to work from the office 2-3 times a week with prior notice, along with attending virtual and in-person client interactions as part of your core responsibilities.

Given the nature of the role, adherence to scheduled meetings, events, and assigned responsibilities is critical. Failure to meet commitments without prior communication may lead to performance review and necessary action.

Compensation Structure

Your stipend will be structured as follows:

  • ₹7,000 for the first month
  • From the second month onwards:
  • ₹10,000 (Remote engagement)
  • ₹15,000 (Hybrid - If actively attending client meetings and events)

The stipend may further increase based on performance, including revenue contribution, deal closures, and overall business impact.

Upon successful completion of the internship and subject to performance, you may be considered for a full-time opportunity. The full-time compensation structure will include a revised fixed component along with uncapped performance-based incentives linked to revenue generation, deal closures, and pipeline ownership.

Role Scope & Learning Exposure

This role is structured to accelerate your growth in B2B and enterprise sales. You will work on the end-to-end deal lifecycle (lead → pitch → closure), understand client requirements, align solutions, and support go-to-market strategies.

You will operate in a training-led, high-performance environment with continuous assessments, mentorship, and hands-on exposure to real business scenarios.

Work Expectations & Performance Standards

You will be expected to work Monday to Saturday (6 days a week), with flexible working hours aligned to business requirements. Your engagement will be evaluated on consistency in meeting participation, ownership and quality of execution, responsiveness in communication, and timely submission of daily updates.

Failure to attend scheduled meetings, events, or assigned responsibilities without prior approval may lead to performance review and stipend adjustments.

Attendance & Leave Policy

Planned leaves must be communicated at least 7 days in advance. Uninformed or unapproved leaves may result in loss of pay and may lead to further action in case of repeated occurrences.

Travel & Reimbursements

This role may require travel for client meetings and events. Any reimbursements, if applicable, will be governed by company policy and approved at the company's discretion.

Payment Terms

Stipend payments will be processed between the 7th and 15th of the subsequent month and credited to your registered bank account.

Confidentiality & Intellectual Property

You are required to maintain strict confidentiality of all company and client-related information. Any breach may result in immediate termination and legal action.

All work created during your engagement will remain the intellectual property of Banao Technologies, and this obligation will continue even after your tenure ends.

Termination Clause

The company reserves the right to terminate this engagement at any time based on performance, conduct, or business requirements. You may choose to discontinue the internship by providing a minimum 7 days' prior notice.
