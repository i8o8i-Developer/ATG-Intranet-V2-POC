ANNEXURE – 1C

1. INTERNS - WORK TIMINGS, ETHICS AND RESPONSIBILITIES

You will be required to adhere to the work timings, ethics and responsibilities as mentioned in Annexure - 1B. Failure to consistently meet these criterias will be considered as a work ethics breach and may result in loss of pay and you being put on probation (Annexure 1B - point 2).

2. INTERNS - PERFORMANCE OFFER PAY CRITERIA

Interns are compensated with either performance based or fixed pay. Usually the interns join with a performance based offer and graduate to a part-time fixed pay offer towards the end of the internship. Please check your compensation table or ask the HR for clarity.

For a performance based offer, you have to achieve the minimum number of tasks. The minimum payout will be done once the minimum tasks are achieved. Any additional tasks finished beyond the minimum tasks/payout will also be compensated as per the performance component. E.g. an intern with performance offer of Rs. 5000 (500/task) who finishes 12 tasks in a month will be paid Rs. 6000 (500*12) at the end of the month.

In case the minimum tasks are not completed in a month, the management reserves the right to postpone the payment to the next month or disperse the partial/incomplete payment. Internship is considered finished only if the intern finishes the number of minimum tasks for their internship duration. E.g. For minimum tasks = 10 for internship duration = 6 months, you have to finish at least 60 tasks during your internship tenure.

The tasks are usually bugs, branches pushed, testing tasks, or content docs. Please get clarity from your manager at the time of your joining on the tasks. There may be additional bounty on a particular task, thereby multiplying the tasks by that bounty. E.g. a task with bounty 2 will be calculated as 2 tasks.

NOTE : In the first month, the intern most likely works on minor issues, thus pay will be less during the initial 1-2 months. As the intern progresses and learns the skills, he/she will be able to resolve more bounty bugs which can result in a rise in pay. Hikes will be provided on the offer if the intern shows great commitment and zeal to learn and deliver.

Payroll will start only after you are onboarded on to a project. Candidates on bench will not paid

3. INTERNS - FIXED OFFER PAY CRITERIA

Interns on fixed pay will be paid on a pro-rata basis for the number of days they were present in the monthly calendar. E.g. If your fixed pay / month is Rs. 8000 and you were on approved leaves for 10 days, you will be paid ⅔ of Rs. 8000 for the month.

Payroll will start only after you are onboarded on to a project. Candidates on bench will not paid.

4. INTERNS - CERTIFICATE AND LOR

Certificates are only provided at the end of your internship cycle only after your manager’s approval.

The offers cannot be used as a successful completion. For the outcome of the completion of this internship, please email at admin@banao.tech from your official email.

Any leaves taken during the internship duration will extend the internship duration by those number of days.

LORs are rarely provided to exceptional candidates only and are not promised as part of the internship.

Should an intern want to continue the internship after the internship cycle, an internship completion certificate will NOT be provided. However, an ongoing internship certificate can be provided which will state that the intern is presently pursuing an internship with the company for last X months. The ongoing internship certificate will only be provided if you are on active payroll of the company, i.e. you are not on bench or probation.

Company is not liable and will not sign any certificate documents from college.
